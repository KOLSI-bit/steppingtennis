Who We Are

Bridging Tradition and Innovation

Stepping Tennis Foundation, founded by Sthembiso William Maake, is dedicated to preserving the cultural heritage of Alexandra, Johannesburg, while innovating through a unique sport born from the streets.

  • Transforming ukuteka, a traditional street game, into a formalized sport.
  • Empowering youth and fostering social cohesion through sports.
  • Promoting global recognition of Stepping Tennis as a symbol of resilience and creativity.

Our Founder

Meet the visionary behind Stepping Tennis, Sthembiso William Maake, whose passion and creativity transformed a childhood game into a global movement.
